Cyber Security Challenge Germany 2021 Qualification CTF

NFITS e.V. is looking forward to host this year's CSCG.

When?

Begin: Mon, 01 Mar 2021 17:00 UTC
End: Mon, 31 May 2021 17:00 UTC

Where?

The CTF is open to everyone and can be played online. However, there will be participant categories which targets german students and young professionals. A Discord account will be required to sign into the event.

What?

The CSCG provides challenges in the field of IT security, like reverse engineering, binary exploitation, cryptography, web security and misc. Both beginners and experienced CTF players will find challenging tasks in varying difficulty levels.
