Cyber Security Challenge Germany 2025 Qualifiers

NFITS e.V. is looking forward to host this year's CSCG.

When?

Begin: Sat, 01 Mar 2025 18:00 Central European Time
End: Tue, 01 May 2025 18:00 Central European Summer Time

Where?

The CTF is open to everyone and can be played online. However, there will be participant categories which target German students and young professionals. A Discord account will be required to sign into the event.

From soon on you can reach our CTF platform under the following link: play.cscg.live


Communication to other participants and the organizers takes place via discord:
Enter discord

What?

The CSCG provides challenges in the field of IT security, like reverse engineering, binary exploitation, cryptography, web security and misc. Both beginners and experienced CTF players will find challenging tasks in varying difficulty levels.
